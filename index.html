About me

Welcome visitor. My name is Christos Dalamagkas and I am an Informatics and Telecommunications Engineering graduate from the University of Western Macedonia. I like to deal with many topics related to Computer Science and Telecommunication, focusing on science and IT engineering.

Currently, I am PhD candidate at the University of Western Macedonia, focusing on optical networks and optimization. Also, I contribute as researcher to the SPEAR project, funded by the European Commission and Horizon 2020 programme. In parallel, I am editor of educational material at the University of Western Macedonia, I am studying for the CCNA certification and I am a freelancer ICT engineer (VAT registered).
